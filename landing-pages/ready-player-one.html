Intro

In Honors English 11, my class and I read the pop-culture hit novel Ready Player One written by Ernest Cline. After we finished the reading, we were asked to write an MLA essay covering either a topic regarding the struggle between online life and reality seen in the book, or whether it should be integrated into high school curriculums.
